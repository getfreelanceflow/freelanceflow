CORE “MUST ADD” FEATURES

1. 📄 Proposal Templates (BIG upgrade)

Let users choose a starting style:

* Web Design Proposal
* Video Editing Proposal
* Logo Design Proposal
* App Development Proposal
* Social Media Management Proposal
* “High-ticket client” template
* “Upwork quick reply” template

👉 This makes outputs instantly better without users thinking.

⸻

2. 🎯 Job-to-Proposal Auto Fill

Paste a job post → AI extracts:

* client name
* budget (if available)
* pain points
* required skills
* urgency level

Then auto-fills proposal.

👉 This is a HUGE conversion booster.

⸻

3. ⚡ “Hook Generator” (FIRST 2 LINES ONLY)

Add a special tool that generates:

* 3 different opening hooks
* attention-grabbing intros
* problem-based openers

Example:

“I noticed your website is losing visitors due to slow mobile speed…”

👉 Freelancers win or lose in the first 2 lines.

⸻

4. 📊 Proposal Strength Score

AI rates each proposal:

* Clarity
* Persuasion
* Personalization
* Confidence
* Structure

Then shows:

“82/100 — strong, but needs more personalization”

👉 This makes it addictive + improves output quality.

⸻

5. 🧠 Client Tone Detector

Detect job post tone:

* Formal
* Startup
* Corporate
* Casual
* High budget / low budget vibe

Then adjust proposal automatically.

⸻

6. 💬 One-Click “Fix My Proposal”

Button that:

* improves grammar
* makes it more persuasive
* shortens or expands
* removes fluff

👉 This becomes your most used button.

⸻

🔥 DIFFERENTIATION FEATURES (VERY IMPORTANT)

7. 💰 “Price Suggestion AI”

Suggest:

* low / medium / premium pricing
* based on job complexity + niche

Even if rough, it helps beginners a lot.

⸻

8. ⏱️ “Delivery Time Generator”

AI suggests:

* realistic timeline
* faster option upsell (“rush delivery” idea)

⸻

9. 🧲 CTA Optimizer

End proposals with better CTAs like:

* “Let’s hop on a quick call”
* “I can start today if you’re ready”
* “Want me to send a quick mockup?”

⸻

10. 🧾 Portfolio Snippet Inserter

AI pulls from user portfolio and inserts:

* relevant past work
* case study snippet
* proof of experience
